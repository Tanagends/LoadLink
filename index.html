What We Do

We bridge the gap between business loads and transportation means, trucks, optimizing for the lowest business costs and efficient truck routing.
As such, both, businesses and truck owners can use LoadLink to skyrocket their efficiency in a future-esque fashion.

For Businesses with transportation needs

Businesses lower down their transportation overheads with just the click of a button. They gain valuable AI match-up recommendations: factoring in their budget, load type, truck availability—and reliability across several metrics—destination and several other considerations that fine-tune to your unique needs. They can manage and track loads in real time, and exploit cost-efficient compound loads by a single transporter whose route satisfies other outside loads.

For Truck owners and other transport providers

  • Increased visibility and demand
  • Optimized routes and loads
  • streamlined operations
  • Imporoved cash flow
  • Access to data insights
  • Better communication and support
  • Cost Savings

Our Mission

Our mission is to make transportation simpler, smarter, and more accessible for all, while creating a positive impact on the enviroment and the communities we serve.

Our Vision

Our Vision at LoadLink is to revolutionize the logistics industry by creating a seamless and efficient platform that connects truck owners with clients, fostering a reliable, cost-effictive, and sustainable transportation ecosystem. We aim to:

  • Empower truck owners to maximize their capacity and revenue
  • Provide clients with a streamlined and transparent shipping experience
  • Drive innovation and sustainability in the industry through technology and data insights
  • Build a community of trust, reliability, and collaboration between all stackholders
